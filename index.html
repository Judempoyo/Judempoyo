About Me

I am a dedicated web developer with a strong passion for back-end development and creating innovative digital experiences. My expertise lies in building robust server-side solutions using PHP and MySQL, particularly with the Laravel framework, where I apply MVC and OOP principles to develop scalable applications.

In mobile development, I utilize Flutter to create cross-platform applications that deliver seamless user experiences. On the front end, I leverage Tailwind CSS to design responsive and visually appealing interfaces that enhance usability and engagement.
